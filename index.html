Built to be safe, not just available

Trust is the actual product.

Every rule below is enforced, not just stated — at signup, during conversations, and after they end.

ID verification, before anyone listens

Every listener submits a government ID and a photo match before going live on the platform. No exceptions.

Platonic conversations, strictly

Romantic or sexual requests, from either side, are against the rules. Reports are reviewed and repeat or serious violations end in a permanent ban.

Block & report in one tap

Visible on every screen during a session. Reports go to a human safety reviewer, not an algorithm that auto-dismisses them.

No recording without consent

Conversations are never recorded or stored unless both people explicitly agree — and it's never required to use Contigo.

18+ only

Age verification happens at signup for both talkers and listeners. Underage accounts are removed on detection.

A real safety team

Reports are reviewed by people, not buried in a queue. Listeners go through onboarding guidelines before their first session.

For listeners

Get paid to genuinely listen.

If you're someone people naturally open up to, this turns that into income — on your schedule, within clear boundaries.

Getting started

  1. Apply with a government ID and a short photo/video verification.
  2. Complete Contigo's listening guidelines module (about 20 minutes).
  3. Set your own availability and the topics you're comfortable with.
  4. Go live — get matched, talk, get paid per completed session.

What's expected of you

  1. Show up as yourself — present, attentive, honest.
  2. Keep every conversation platonic, full stop.
  3. Report anything that makes you uncomfortable — you're protected for doing so.
  4. End any session that crosses a line. You're never obligated to stay.
Not allowed, ever: romantic or sexual conversation, requests to move off-platform, sharing personal contact details. Violations are reported and can result in a permanent ban for either side.

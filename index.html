Safety by design

Trust is the actual product.

Every rule below is enforced — not aspirational. At signup, during conversations, and after they end.

ID verification before anyone listens

Every listener submits a government ID and a photo match before going live. No exceptions, no shortcuts.

Platonic conversations, without exception

Romantic or sexual requests — from either party — are against the rules. Confirmed violations result in a permanent ban.

Block and report in one tap

Visible on every screen during a session. Reports are reviewed by a real person — not dismissed by an automated filter.

No recording without mutual consent

Conversations are never stored or recorded unless both parties explicitly agree. It is never a requirement to use Contigo.

18+ only

Age is verified at signup for both sides. Underage accounts are removed on detection.

Human review on every report

A real safety team reviews reports. Listeners complete a guidelines module before their first session. Safety is not automated here.

For listeners

Get paid to genuinely listen.

If you're someone people naturally open up to, Contigo turns that into real income — on your schedule, within clear and enforced boundaries.

Getting started

  1. Apply with a government ID and a short photo verification.
  2. Complete Contigo's listening guidelines module — about 20 minutes.
  3. Set your own availability and the topics you are comfortable with.
  4. Go live, get matched, talk, and earn per completed session.

What we expect

  1. Show up as yourself — present, attentive, honest.
  2. Keep every conversation platonic. Always.
  3. Report anything that makes you uncomfortable — you are protected for doing so.
  4. End any session that crosses a line. You are never obligated to continue.
Never permitted: romantic or sexual conversation, requests to move off-platform, sharing personal contact information. Violations result in a permanent ban — for either party.
